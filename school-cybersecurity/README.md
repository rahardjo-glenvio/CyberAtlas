# School Cybersecurity

Security awareness and digital safety in educational environments.

Schools and universities are among the most targeted institutions in cybersecurity incidents: they hold sensitive data about minors, operate on limited security budgets, and depend on open network architectures for their educational mission. This section addresses security from the perspective of students, teachers, and institutions.

## Why Schools Are a Specific Target

Educational institutions face a distinct threat profile:

- Large volumes of student PII (personally identifiable information), including minors
- Financial data: tuition, financial aid, payroll
- Research data with intellectual property value
- Open networks designed for usability, not security
- Limited IT and security staffing relative to institution size
- High turnover of users (students change every year)
- Pressure to remain accessible and not restrict student experience

Understanding this context matters for designing defenses that are appropriate for the environment.
