# Explore

Cybersecurity at the intersection of technology, society, and human behavior.

This section covers topics that sit beyond the purely technical — the social, political, economic, and behavioral dimensions of security. These topics matter for anyone who wants to understand cybersecurity as a phenomenon, not just a skill set.

## Why This Section Exists

Security incidents are not just technical failures. They are human events with human causes and human consequences.

Understanding social engineering requires understanding psychology. Understanding ransomware requires understanding criminal economics. Understanding privacy requires understanding data collection incentives. Understanding deepfakes requires understanding trust and media.

Technical defenses fail when the people and organizations using them do not understand the environment they are operating in. This section provides that context.
