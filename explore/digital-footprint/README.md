# Digital Footprint

What you leave behind online — and how it is collected, aggregated, and used.

A digital footprint is the trail of data created by online activity. Most of it is generated passively — without any deliberate action from the user. Understanding the scope of this data, who collects it, and how it is used is a prerequisite for any meaningful privacy decision.
