# Digital Privacy

What data is collected, by whom, and what individuals can do about it.

Privacy is not the same as security. Security protects data from unauthorized access. Privacy governs who has authorized access, for what purpose, and whether individuals consent to or even know about it. Both matter — and they often intersect.
