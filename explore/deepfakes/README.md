# Deepfakes

Synthetic media: technology, threats, and the challenge of trust.

Deepfakes are AI-generated synthetic media that realistically depict people saying or doing things they did not say or do. The technology is not new, but its accessibility and quality have changed the calculus of trust in digital media, corporate communications, and personal reputation.
