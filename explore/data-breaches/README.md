# Data Breaches

What actually happens in a breach: beyond the headlines.

Data breaches are reported in the news with aggregate numbers (millions of records, billions of dollars) but rarely explained in terms of what actually happened, what the data means in practice, and what the long-term effects are for individuals and organizations.
